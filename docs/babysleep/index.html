Happy Baby, Happy Mother; Happy Mother, Happy Life

This app plays sounds to calm down and put asleep babies. With autoplay on motion and noise events, it saves your sleep.

"It plays more than 10 different sounds that have been carefully selected by experts and tested with parental experience, for your baby to sleep well."

Sound Sense

When there is a sound (when the baby cries or there is noise), it can play the automatically selected sound.

Motion Sense

It can play the selected sound automatically upon movement.

Record Feature

You can record your own voice. Record your own lullaby and put your child to sleep with the mother's voice.

Record Sharing

You can share your favorite sound with your loved ones.

Background Feature

It plays sounds in the background, you can turn off the phone screen or browse other applications on your phone while your baby is falling asleep.

Customizable

You can put your baby's own picture on the background, choose a color profile according to its gender.

Baby Sleep-Twinkle White Noise

More than 10 baby sleep sounds including pink & white noise, womb.


Play the sounds as long as you want. If you wish, let the sound repeat constantly.
